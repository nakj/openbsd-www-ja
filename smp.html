SMP

History and Status:

The project to add SMP support to OpenBSD was started in February, 2000.

As of June, 2004, SMP (Symmetric MultiProcessor) support has been merged into the main OpenBSD development branch. At this time, the i386, amd64, mvme88k, sparc64 and macppc platforms supports SMP. The sgi port supports SMP on the multiprocessor Octane models, other machines aren't support (yet). Hopefully other platforms, including hppa and sparc will ultimately support SMP.

Please see the appropriate platform page for SMP status for your system.
